Bowl of Cereal

Description

The "Bowl of Cereal" is a classic dish renowned for its ease of preparation, and beloved by most children.

Ingredients

  • Breakfast cereal, e.g. Cheerios
  • Milk

Recipe

  1. Pour desired amount of breakfast cereal into a bowl.
  2. Pour milk over breakfast cereal until mixture becomes slightly raised, making sure to restrict the pour to one spot so as to prevent all the cereal from becoming soggy.
